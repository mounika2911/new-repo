COGNITUS INTELLIGENT SPEND MANAGEMENT POWERED BY ARIBA

Today, Supply Chain Leaders have to weigh spending on growth against controlling spend as they respond to the demands of a fast-moving digital and global economy.

Spend management is the tried and true practice of comprehensively managing all supplier relationships and company purchasing to identify every dollar spent and get the most out of it. Spend management best practices integrate and automate all spend-related activities from source to settle so that buying happens as planned and suppliers get paid in compliance with contracts.

SAP ARIBA BUYING AND INVOICING (PROCURE TO PAY – P2P)

SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing is the easy-to-use, procure-to-pay (P2P) software solution that sets the standard for all other procurement software offerings. It’s user-friendly, with streamlined catalogue maintenance and a configurable interface. This P2P solution can help your business become more agile by allowing you to better control global spend with flexible management and visibility tools. SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing also helps you to simplify commerce by consolidating and managing each phase of your procure-to-pay process.

Buyers from large, midsize, and growing companies enjoy the simple and smart guided buying experience. It delivers a quick ROI through built-in approval flows that ensure both catalogue and contract compliance. The result: you can manage more spend with less effort – helping you maximize savings and meet unprecedented demands with intelligence, innovation, and speed.

SAP ARIBA SUPPLY CHAIN COLLABORATION

ARIBA SNAP

Designed specifically for mid-sized companies SAP Ariba SNAP allows growing companies to adopt integrated procure to pay solutions, improve cashflow compliance and control and scales as they grow. By digitalizing and automating each step of their buying process, growing organizations eliminate paper-intensive, manual processes that prevent them from gaining visibility into company-wide spending. Using Ariba SNAP’s role-based dashboards that present real-time information, procurement leaders get the actionable insights they need to control spending and reduce costs

Backed by SAP Ariba’s decades of procurement expertise, SAP Ariba SNAP provides easy-to-use tools for both you and your employees through a solution that is:

  • Packaged and priced to fit your business.

  • Built to improve cash flow, control, and compliance without adding complexity.

  • Designed to scale as you grow.

  • Capable of freeing up your staff to focus on more strategic sourcing and procurement activities.
