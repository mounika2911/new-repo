COMPANY OVERVIEW

Cognitus is a global firm specializing in SAP packaged solutions, including
consultancy and SAP S/4HANA migration for digital transformation. Our
people consist of SAP veterans from the days of R/2, with experience in
everything from the challenges of early SAP to complex S/4HANA
migrations. That’s why our customers trust us with their mission-critical
projects.

Today, Cognitus is a global firm, employing 1000+ people. Those people
bring business acumen, technical knowhow, and a holistic approach to
services, support, and transitions. It is our vast experience, as well as our
packaged solutions and factory approach, which distinguishes us from our
competitors.

Cognitus EMEA launched in 2020 to deliver a full-service SAP consulting
and support. That includes the factory-migration and support offered by
Cognitus, with the backing of our global delivery and support centers.

Most importantly, we invest in SAP expertise, with partners, consultants,
and executives who have the SAP expertise to drive innovation, growth,
and smooth technical transitions. Our focus is on delivering global support,
with a local presence and touch.

VISION, MISSION & CORE VALUES

Vision

We strive to be the trusted go-to Enterprise
transformation solutions company by providing
industry-leading solutions.

Mission

Our Mission is to solve the digital challenges of
our clients by being a partner that provides
qualified intelligent industry solutions and
expert advice to the SAP Ecosystem in a cost
effective and sustainable way. And create value
for our customers, while improving the quality
of life for our employees and returning to
society through world class advice.
