COGNITUS EXPERTISE IN SAP S/4HANA SOLUTIONS

Gallop™ is the fastest, revolutionary and the most economical solution for businesses to convert to SAP S/4HANA. Gallop™ is a collection of two distinct packages that help you accelerate to SAP S/4HANA, Gallop™ Pilot and Gallop™ Deployment.

SAP Aerospace and Defense Industry Cloud Solution by Cognitus

Adopted by leading A&D customers Gallop Defense Contract Lifecycle Management is Aerospace and Defense Industry Cloud solution that address challenges faced by businesses working with the Department of Defense in being DCAA, DCMA, FAR, DFARS, CAS compliant, makes Ariba cloud offering FedRAMP compliant and enables sustainability / carbon footprint reporting.
