INDUSTRIAL MANUFACTURING

(INDUSTRIAL MACHINERY AND COMPONENTS)

The Industrial Machinery and Components industry is at the heart of the ubiquitous digital shift. It provides the intelligent machines and equipment needed for the transformation as well as spearheading of innovative processes such as connected manufacturing and predictive maintenance and service.

Cognitus helps IM&C companies transform to intelligent enterprises through integrated business applications that use intelligent technologies. This enables them to deliver breakthrough business value to their customers by running on next-generation processes.

INTELLIGENT ENTERPRISE SUITE

The Intelligent Enterprise suite is a set of applications providing the business capabilities that IM&C companies need to run their business

IM&C industry end-to-end business processes can span multiple applications, so processes and data must be integrated for automation to gain a seamless user experience, fast adoption, and ease of operations.

SAP S/4HANA provides a platform for extending the business processes of the Intelligent Suite and enabling new innovations. Innovation services combining design-thinking and industry accelerators ensure customers derive value from innovative new technologies quickly and with reduced risk.

CUSTOMIZED PROCESSES WITH INTELLIGENT TECHNOLOGIES

Technological advancements in IoT, Big Data, artificial intelligence and automation can open paths to customizing business processes in multiple variations for a tailor-made solution for both discrete and process manufacturing.

Several innovative technologies have matured to practical use:

  • The Internet of Things makes business applications interact with the physical world.
  • Machine learning and artificial intelligence automate repetitive processes and learn from human exception handling and decision-making.

Advanced analytics find data patterns to support decisions and predict the future:

  • Ability to gather massive amounts of data and use it for real time insights and reactions
  • Blockchain distributes collaborative processes across the entire value network.
  • Data intelligence finds new value in data assets for new business models.

DIGITAL PLATFORM

The digital platform, which is powered by SAP HANA, extends the intelligent end-to-end processes and connects to data sources:

  • Cloud platform that allows customers and partners to extend their intelligent suite to run additional business processes
  • Data management to handle and organize data, a key asset of an intelligent enterprise
