PARTNERS

OUR PARTNERS

To bring the best, proven digital solutions to our customers, we have integrated several market leading solutions that further enhance the SAP capabilities.
We have partnered with prestigious organizations who are each trendsetters in their fields. Together we will continue to bring to you the latest upgrades,
cloud-native applications and technology advancements.

SAP

SAP has set the gold standard for ERP software and is used by the largest organizations in the world. It can enhance the organizations’ ability to reach new markets, accelerate revenue, increase customer retention and improve business capabilities. With the next technological movements being AI and machine learning, SAP has already developed programs utilizing these capabilities. As an end-to-end SAP systems integrator and one of the select few partners for SAP’s Move to SAP S/4HANA initiative, Cognitus is providing the best SAP has to offer to our customers, through innovation and digital transformation, driven by our experienced consultants. We are now bringing the latest offering from SAP, RISE with SAP, that bundles the essentials of an intelligent enterprise to our customers.

PRICE FX

Pricefx delivers a complete price optimization and management platform based on native cloud architecture, providing industry leading time to value and total cost of ownership advantages to customers. Their versatile solution works for both B2B and B2C enterprises of any size, in any industry, in any part of the world. Cognitus has taken this a step further by integrating this with SAP S/4HANA and C/4HANA to provide our customers’ sales teams with an intelligent platform that serves as a single source for pricing data and strategies. It is an innovative platform that combines SAP’s digital core capabilities, Pricefx’ world-class pricing solution and Cognitus’ expertise as a systems integrator to solve future and current pricing problems.

CELONIS

Celonis can help you leverage your business data to gain objective, fact-based insights derived from actual event logs that help you audit, analyze, and improve your existing business processes by answering both compliance-related and performance-related questions. Celonis is an automated cloud-based process mining platform that plots, records, tracks and analyzes each component of your business process. To bring the advantages of process mining to our customers Cognitus teamed up with Celonis to offer Celonis’ Process Explorer as part of our SAP S/4HANA assessment package, Gallop Pilot. SAP customers can now get a clearer picture of the gaps in their current processes and to-be processes, to help them decide the right approach to migration.

NATUVION

Natuvion is one of the 4 SAP partners to provide Selective Data Transition to SAP customers. This gives customers moving to SAP S/4HANA, the opportunity of a flexible redesign of business processes and reuse of data without complications during transition. Cognitus has partnered with Natuvion to provide this powerful capability to our SAP customers as an inherent part of the Gallop portfolio that is designed for accelerated deployment. With this, the carve-outs can be implemented on the most granular level, businesses will have the option to select the instance or the data they would like to move, preserve current functionality that they prefer to keep while leveraging the advanced capabilities of SAP S/4HANA. Gallop deployment powered by Natuvion, will simultaneously enable optimization of your entire system landscape and ensure a transition approach that meets your time and budget goals.

LIVEHIRE

LiveHire, is a leading recruitment and talent mobility software company based in Australia. Their award-winning talent acquisition platform has transformed the traditional recruitment process into a digital, new age process reducing the hiring time cycle significantly. In partnership with LiveHire, Cognitus has innovatively integrated SAP SuccessFactors & LiveHire Talent Management platform for a seamless experience to attract, engage and hire the best talent through one of the best dedicated HRIS systems in the industry. An intuitive and easy to use AI-powered application it grows your private talent cloud of prospective candidates.

KYMANOX

Life sciences companies face many complexities of working in a highly regulated environment. Kymanox and Cognitus have come together to alleviate this pain point by combining Kymanox’ expertise in validation and compliance; and Cognitus’ deep, hands on experience with SAP ERP to provide a patient-centric approach to the SAP ecosystem. Cognitus’ solution Gallop Life sciences powered by Kymanox combines SAP S/4HANA’s intelligent technologies, industry focused best practices and automatic regulatory compliance for pharmaceutical, healthcare organizations and medical device manufacturers. It offers real time insights to digital supply chain that is both adaptable and transparent and connects hospitals, patients, distributors and government agencies

REV-TRAC

Change in your business is inevitable and necessary, but will be beneficial only if managed proactively. According to a widely accepted study, 70% of change management activities fail. Rev-Trac and Cognitus’ strategic partnership can help mitigate this risk, by delivering to you an automated SAP change management solution. Rev-Trac is an out of the box ABAP based SAP change & transport management, automation, collaboration, mobility, reporting and audit software suite. It can help manage change, prevent error and speed up the process from the development of the change, to the production of your products and services. Cognitus experts will be the single point of contact for integrating Rev-Trac and SAP functionality and drive continuous improvement.

UIPATH

To be a leading company in today’s environment, flexibility, adaptability and digitalization are a must. Automation fulfills these 3 key components and has become an essential capability that requires precision and expertise. Cognitus proudly partners with UiPath, a consecutive winning title holder in the 2020 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Robotic Process Automation to help organizations solve problems and face the many challenges presented in today’s world. From discovery powered by AI, building and managing automation, running the programs with your applications and engaging AI and people, Cognitus is here to guide your journey and help you gain full value from UiPath.

SERVICENOW

Cognitus provides its customers the all-encompassing ServiceNow’s platform for IT service management and streamlining workflows across various departments. ServiceNow is an enterprise cloud solution for IT services management (ITSM), IT operations management (ITOM) and IT business management (ITBM). Organizations can manage projects, teams and customer interactions all consolidated in one platform. It is easily scalable, easily configured allowing users to set up and start quickly. Cognitus’ customers can benefit from this digital, cloud based, software as a service for AMS, technical management support and to processes and catalog customer service requests.

JIRA BY ATLASSIAN

JIRA is a project management and an incident management tool that can be integrated with a service desk to issue alerts and trigger workflows, providing 24/7 coverage. Cognitus uses JIRA for its internal project management and also offers this platform to its PCoE and AMS customers. JIRA is used to define, maintain and apply SLA’s, to monitor performance through many KPIs and reports and customize workflows. It comes with pre-configured functionality for commonly reported incidents making it easier and faster for users to log tickets. It can be used for change management as well. Keeping in step with changing project management methodologies, JIRA has been optimized for agile teams.

WINSHUTTLE

Winshuttle takes the mundane out of repetitive tasks by automating data-intensive processes. It is an SAP certified product and a great addition for SAP customers and can improve data quality and accelerate business processes across your SAP landscape including materials, customers, vendors, and finance, with automated solutions that eliminate manual data entry via the SAP GUI. This is a great way to free up your resources for more meaningful and challenging work opportunities. Cognitus has partnered with Winshuttle to deploy this using expert developers. Winshuttle can be deployed both as part of migration or used for Application Management Support projects.
