SAP CERTIFIED APPLICATION MANAGEMENT SERVICES (AMS)

Cognitus is certified by SAP for maintenance, daily operations, and ongoing functional support for business applications for SAP Business Suite, SAP S/4HANA®, and SAP SuccessFactors® solutions. Solution-related, extensive functional SAP application skills and experience is mandatory for this authorization.

AUTHORIZED SAP PARTNER CENTER OF EXPERTISE (PCOE)

Cognitus is an SAP authorized Partner Center of Expertise for providing L2, L3 support in adherence with SAP best practices backed by SAP Enterprise Support.

CERTIFIED FOR SAP HANA OPERATIONS AND HOSTING OPERATIONS

Cognitus is a certified provider of ongoing technical operations of SAP applications and for managing solutions running on SAP HANA.

SAP CERTIFIED TEAM

Our consultants are SAP certified and continuously upskill via SAP Learning Hub

OUR SERVICES COVER THE ENTIRE SAP LIFECYCLE
